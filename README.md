# Trade System App

Trade System App is a community-driven barter and points-based marketplace that allows users to trade **used items, services, and points** in any combination.  
The platform enables flexible, trust-based trading without relying solely on money.

---

## 🚀 Why Trade System App?

Traditional marketplaces force users to buy and sell only with money.  
Trade System App introduces a **flexible any-to-any trade model**, empowering communities to exchange value through barter, services, and points.

Example trades:
- Old bike → Guitar  
- 1hr Guitar Lesson → Home-cooked meal  
- 50 points + old phone → 2 hours of plumbing service  

---

## 🧩 Core Features

### 🛒 Community Marketplace
- List used items or offer services
- Trade using **points, barter, or a mix of both**
- Local and online trades supported

### 💰 Points Wallet
- Every new user receives **300 points on signup**
- Earn points by selling items or offering services
- Spend points to buy or trade
- Points are non-refundable and trade-only

### 📦 Listings (Items & Services)
**Used Items**
- Photos, title, description, condition, and location
- Pricing options:
  - Points only
  - Barter only
  - Points + Barter

**Services**
- Categories (Tutoring, Music, Plumbing, Painting, etc.)
- Availability, rate, and delivery mode (online/in-person)
- Flexible pricing using points or barter

## 🔁 Flexible Trade Engine (Any-to-Any)

The platform supports all trade combinations:

| From \ To | Points | Item | Service |
|---------|--------|------|---------|
| Points  | ❌     | ✅   | ✅      |
| Item   | ✅     | ✅   | ✅      |
| Service| ✅     | ✅   | ✅      |

Supported trades:
- Points → Item / Service
- Item / Service → Points
- Item ↔ Item
- Service ↔ Service
- Item ↔ Service
- Mixed offers (points + item/service)

---

## 🔄 Trade Flow

1. User selects a listing and clicks **Make Offer**
2. Offer can include:
   - Points
   - Items
   - Services
   - Any combination
3. Counter-offers allowed (up to **3 rounds**)
4. Both parties accept → Trade is locked
5. Trade completed (online or in-person)
6. Points and ownership are transferred
7. Users rate each other
